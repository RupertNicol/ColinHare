About Colin Hare

Colin Hare was a replacement member of Honeycombs and toured Israel with them in 1966. Joined Honeybus in 1967 and played on their Hit "I can`t let Maggie go`1968, which remains a classic of the era. After the band`s demise recorded his first solo album "March Hare" which has seen 6 re-issues to date and more in the pipeline.
